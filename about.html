About — Rochan

Student · Builder · Bio + AI Explorer

Who I Am

Rochan is a sophomore at Lambert High School exploring the intersection of synthetic biology, immuno-engineering, computational protein design, and historical linguistics. He likes building weird bridges between disciplines people don’t usually mix — like immune circuits and language models.

What I'm Trying To Do

Design biological systems and computational tools to better understand, predict, and engineer living systems — making biology that’s programmable and code that understands biology.

Current Work

Projects include protein engineering experiments, immune-circuit design, and analysis of emergent structure in AI systems. The work mixes computation, wet-lab planning, and lots of reading.

Beyond Research

Outside of research, he plays cricket, cooks (occasionally catastrophically), competes in Clash Royale, and speed-runs Wikiraces. He also hates writing about himself in the third person.
