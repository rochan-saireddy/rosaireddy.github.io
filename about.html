About Rochan

Student · Builder · Bio + AI Explorer

Who I Am

Rochan is a sophomore at Lambert High School exploring the intersection of synthetic biology, immuno-engineering, computational protein design, and historical linguistics. He likes building weird bridges between disciplines people don’t usually mix — like immune circuits and language models.

What I'm Trying to Do

His focus right now is on designing biological systems and computational tools that help us better understand — and engineer — living systems. Basically: building things biology can run, and things computers can understand about biology.

Current Work

Currently cooking up projects in protein engineering, immune-circuit design, and studying how AI systems evolve. He enjoys the challenge of designing new molecular functions and computational pipelines to analyze biological behavior.

Beyond Science

When he's not buried in projects, he’s out playing cricket, experimenting in the kitchen (sometimes catastrophically), demolishing kids in Clash Royale, and racing Wikipedia pages like it's a sport.

Personality Upload
