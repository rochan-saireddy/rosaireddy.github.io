Vegetable Fish

Brand, mascot, resume hub

Origin Story

Vegetable Fish, or his full name, sir Roger Vegetario Horatio Eggplantson II, began from something I saw in a The Thought Emporium video (Here!), but morphed into an inside joke between me and my friends and has been my personal brand since 2023!

Symbolism & Ethos

Vegetable Fish symbolizes abominable brillinace — making something that really shouldn't exist just for the sake of making it. It's a hybrid in most every sense: plant + animal, lab + kitchen, careful engineering + chaotic play. It’s the mascot for projects that don’t belong to neat boxes yet still get the job done.
