Vegetable Fish

Brand, mascot, resume hub

Origin Story

Vegetable Fish began as a dumb Discord joke and became a personal brand: a hybrid creature that stands for curiosity, messy creativity, and the tension between science and nonsense.

Symbolism & Ethos

Vegetable Fish symbolizes hybridity — plant + animal, lab + kitchen, careful engineering + chaotic play. It’s the mascot for projects that don’t belong to neat boxes.

Resume & Showcase

This page doubles as a hub for your resume and highlight links. Drop a PDF named resume.pdf in the repo root and the nav's resume orb will link to it.
